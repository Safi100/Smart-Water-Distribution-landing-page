The Water Distribution Challenge

Communities worldwide face challenges with water scarcity and unfair allocation. Large users like businesses often overconsume, leaving families with insufficient resources. It's time to rethink how water is managed and distributed.

Our Smart Solution

Our system ensures equitable water distribution based on family size, leveraging advanced sensors, automation, and mobile technology.

Fair Allocation

Real-Time Monitoring

User-Friendly Apps

Discover the Power of Our Website and Mobile App

Our digital solutions bring convenience and control to water distribution management. Designed for families and water authorities alike, these tools simplify monitoring and decision-making.

Web App

The web application is designed for water management administrators, offering a dashboard to:

  • Monitor real-time water usage across all households.
  • Access family size data to ensure fair distribution.
  • Set monthly limits and customize distribution schedules.
  • Generate detailed reports on water consumption trends.
Mobile App

The mobile application is tailored for families to provide:
 

  • A clear view of current water usage and remaining allowance.
  • Notifications about refill schedules or exceeding usage limits.
  • Insights into daily, weekly, and monthly consumption patterns.
  • Secure login and account management for personalized data.
